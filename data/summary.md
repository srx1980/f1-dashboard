# Monaco Grand Prix — Race Summary

**Andrea Kimi Antonelli** made it five wins from seven rounds with a masterclass on the streets of Monte Carlo, converting a front-row start into a commanding victory and extending his championship lead to 14 points.

## Key Moments

- **Lap 1:** Antonelli swept past polesitter Charles Leclerc into Sainte Dévote and never looked back.
- **Lap 22:** Title rival **Max Verstappen** retired with an engine failure, a costly blow to Red Bull's championship hopes.
- **Lap 33–41:** Two separate collisions at the Nouvelle Chicane ended the races of Hadjar and Doohan, bringing out the safety car.
- **Lap 74:** **Lewis Hamilton** set the fastest lap of the race, a 1:13.221, charging back to sixth after a slow first stint.

## Championship Picture

Antonelli now leads Verstappen by 14 points, while **Mercedes** stretches its constructors' advantage over McLaren. With the European triple-header looming, the momentum is firmly with the young Italian.
